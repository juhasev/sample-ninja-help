# Exit feedback

Exit feedback is voluntarily collected when a panelist decides to unsubscribe. Panelists can type whatever they want in the feedback box. 

## Word cloud
Sample Ninja takes this feedback and applies an advanced machine learning algorithm to extract meaningful statements and their frequencies. This chart gives you an idea of what most panelists are talking about.

## Sentiment analysis and recent feedback
Sample Ninja displays the last 100 feedback along with their sentiment so you can inspect them individually. If you have more than 100 feedback comments then click on the LOAD MORE button to load the next 100 panelists. The results are always ordered with the latest one first.

> The following languages are supported: en, ers, fr, de, it, pt, ar, hi, ja, ko, zh are for AI analysis; otherwise, the word cloud will simply revert to keyword counts and display the most frequent words appearing in the text. The AI analysis is more advanced, taking into consideration the most used phrases and their context.

## To view or edit panelist
Click anywhere in the feedback box.
