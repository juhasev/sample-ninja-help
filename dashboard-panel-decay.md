## Panel Decay

This widget highlights the panelists who are decaying out of your reachable, active audience, split into three groups:

- **Bounced email** — panelists who are still subscribed, but whose email address has bounced or been flagged as invalid. Your emails are no longer reaching them. This slice is pulled out to draw your attention.
- **Unsubscribed** — panelists who have opted out, or were unsubscribed by an administrator.
- **Blacklisted** — panelists who have been blacklisted.

> Panelists with bounced email still count toward your panel size, but can no longer be reached by email. Consider manually unsubscribing them or attempting to recover them via an alternative contact route, such as telephone, push notification, or an updated email address.

> All panelists with an unsubscribed and blacklisted status will be deleted automatically from your panel in 1 year or 12 months. All panelists in these categories will have their **PI (Personally Identifiable)** data automatically deleted in accordance with the **Data Retention Policy**, with the default being **31 days**. After this period, recovery is difficult because the panelist's name, email, and all custom variables are deleted. Panelists' activity history is retained for 12 months for analytical purposes.

> To review you current **Data Renention Policy** please visit **Settings->Basic Settings (tab) -> Data Retention Policy**

> **IMPORTANT** Do not delete your panelists, or you will have a skewed picture of the past 12 months. Measurements like panel size, number of completes, churn, and others will be affected.
